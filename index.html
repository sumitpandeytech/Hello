ABOUT US

REDX-CGU is a Non-profitable and anti-disciplinary club that is supported and guided by MIT Media Lab and serves as gateways to the energy and creativity of innovators who are creating solutions to satisfy significant social needs.

Under the supervision of Prof. Cher-Ming Tan, we will have opportunity to work closely with Engineers, corporate executives, academics from institutions of higher learning, government officials and experienced mentors.
